About The Event

Join us for the APJI 41th Anniversary Food Run, a celebration of running, community, and of course, delicious food! This event marks 41 years of our community's dedication to health, fitness, and camaraderie.

All participants will receive a finisher medal, official race jersey, and access to our post-race food festival featuring local vendors and delicious treats.
